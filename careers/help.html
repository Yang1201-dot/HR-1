Help & Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

From the Careers home page, find a job and click "Apply Now". Complete the form and upload your resume (PDF) and any supporting documents requested.

At minimum, upload a current resume/CV. Depending on the role, you may be asked to upload:

  • Birth certificate (image or PDF)
  • Diploma or Transcript of Records (image or PDF)
  • Cover letter (optional)

If you need to update documents after submission, contact us at careers@microfinance.local with details and we will advise on next steps.

Hiring timelines vary by role. If your application is shortlisted, our HR team will contact you with next steps.
