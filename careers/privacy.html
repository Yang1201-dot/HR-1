Privacy Policy

We value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Microfinance collects, uses, shares and protects the personal information you provide when applying for jobs through our Careers site.

  • Application data you submit (name, email, phone, resume, supporting documents).
  • Technical data such as IP address and browser user agent for security and analytics.

Your information is used to process job applications, communicate with candidates, and for recruitment-related record keeping. We may also use aggregated, non-identifying information for analytics to improve our services.

We retain application records for a period consistent with recruitment needs and applicable law. If you wish to request deletion or correction of your personal data, contact us using the Contact page.

Applicant data may be viewed by authorized members of our HR team and hiring managers. We employ reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal data, but no system can be guaranteed completely secure.

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal data we hold about you. Contact us at careers@microfinance.local for such requests.
